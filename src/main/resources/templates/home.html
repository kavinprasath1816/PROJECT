Eligibility

Nationality:

  • The applicant should be a Resident / Non Resident Indian National.
  • Candidates studied/studying abroad can apply directly through foreign category.

Age Limit :

  • Candidates whose date of birth falls on or after 1st July 2000 are eligible to apply.
  • Date of birth as recorded in the High School / SSC / X Certificate will be considered authentic.
  • Candidates should produce this certificate in original as a proof of their age at the time of counselling / admission, failing which they will be disqualified.
